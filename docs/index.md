## What this documentation covers

A **Service** is a configuration unit that connects a payment Provider with the required Credentials and operational settings.
In this section you will learn how to create, validate, and maintain services in a safe and predictable way.

<div class="callout callout--info">
  <strong>Security and compliance</strong><br/>
  Do not place real card data (PAN, CVV), full bank account numbers, or production secrets in screenshots, examples, or logs.
  Use masked values and placeholders.
</div>
